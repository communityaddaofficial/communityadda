About Us

Built in Odisha, For Odisha

CommunityAdda was founded with one simple belief — that the artisans, farmers, weavers, and small businesses who shape Odisha's economy deserve a marketplace that doesn't take a cut of their hard work. We're building a platform where every seller keeps 100% of what they earn, where local pride meets nationwide reach, and where commerce strengthens community instead of competing with it. This is just the beginning — and we're growing it together with the people who make Odisha what it is.
